Animal Facts

Bear

Bears are large mammals found in forests and tundras, they eat both plants and animals and are known for hibernating during winter.

  • Weight: 300 to 800 kg
  • Length: 1.5 to 2.8 meters
  • Main Diet: Berries, fish, small mammals
  • Life Expectancy: 20 to 30 years

Lion

Lions are powerful predators, known as the "king of the jungle." They live in groups called prides and hunt large animals like antelopes and zebras.

  • Weight: 150 to 250 kg
  • Length: 1.4 to 2.5 meters
  • Main Diet: Antelopes, zebras, wildebeest
  • Life Expectancy: 10 to 14 years

Shark

Sharks are ancient marine predators that maintain balance in the ocean. They are known for their sharp teeth and swift movements.

  • Weight: 200 to 900 kg
  • Length: 1.5 to 6 meters
  • Main Diet: Fish, seals, smaller sharks
  • Life Expectancy: 20 to 30 years
